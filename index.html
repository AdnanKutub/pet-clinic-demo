Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept pet insurance?

Yes! We work with all major pet insurance providers including Trupanion, Nationwide, and Healthy Paws. We can help you submit your claims.

Do you offer emergency services?

We handle urgent care during business hours. For after-hours emergencies, we refer to the 24/7 Animal Hospital on Main St.

How often should my pet see the vet?

We recommend annual wellness exams for adult pets and bi-annual exams for seniors (7+ years) to catch age-related issues early.
